Quick Answers

Common contact topics

What details should I add for shipment support?

Include shipment ID, pickup city, delivery city, vehicle number, current status, and the exact issue you want checked.

Who handles stock mismatch requests?

Inventory support reviews SKU count, inward records, dispatch scans, damaged stock notes, and warehouse reconciliation.

Can vendors contact Stackly for payment updates?

Yes. Vendors can share invoice number, GST details, PO reference, and delivery proof with the vendor helpdesk.

How fast are urgent tickets handled?

Critical shipment and fleet issues are routed to the control room first, then escalated to regional operations when needed.
